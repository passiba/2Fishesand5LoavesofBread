2 Fishes and 5 Loaves of Bread

The Game is aming at feeding the swimming fishes around the brad basket to be fed whenever there are pieces of bread floating around in wather. The aim of this game is to feed as many fishes with pieces of bread in order for them to stay alive. In a way the Fishes represent the new-born-belivers in Jesus Christ and the bread basket represents the body of Jesus Christ,who died for our sins and by sharing the Christian communion people share their faith together with other swimming fishes,members of the living body of united global Body of Jesus Christ. Our defender,Holy Spirit is also there to help us to enrich our daily lives. Change this so it covers your game. Remember to descibe the game and contact e-mail. The HTML5 Game Starter Kit is a starting point for you who would like to create games for Windows 8. It includes the basic functionality you need for creating games that will be published in Windows Store.

It includes adding an About page and a Privacy Policy page on the Settings-charm, snap view support, HTML5/JavaScript canvas for game rendering, tiles and touch input.
